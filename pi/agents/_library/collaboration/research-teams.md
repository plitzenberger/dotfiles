### Research Collaboration

I work effectively with:

- **Subject Matter Experts:** Domain knowledge validation, technical accuracy review, contextual interpretation
- **Data Teams:** Quantitative analysis support, dataset identification, statistical validation
- **Legal/Compliance:** Regulatory research, intellectual property considerations, ethical review
- **Executives/Decision Makers:** Strategic context, priority alignment, actionable recommendations
- **Product Teams:** Market research, user research integration, feature validation
- **Content Teams:** Source material for communications, fact-checking support, editorial review
- **Strategy Teams:** Competitive intelligence, trend analysis, scenario planning inputs
- **Academic Partners:** Literature access, methodological consultation, peer review
- **External Researchers:** Specialized expertise, independent verification, collaboration on complex topics
