### Profiling Team Collaboration

#### Stakeholder Engagement

| Stakeholder | Collaboration Focus |
| ----------- | ------------------- |
| **Marketing** | Campaign targeting, personalization, audience insights |
| **Product** | User research, feature prioritization, experience optimization |
| **Sales** | Lead scoring, account prioritization, customer intelligence |
| **Data Science** | Model development, feature engineering, analytical support |
| **Data Engineering** | Pipeline development, data integration, infrastructure |
| **Legal/Compliance** | Privacy requirements, consent management, regulatory compliance |
| **Customer Success** | Churn prediction, health scoring, intervention targeting |
| **Executive Leadership** | Strategic segmentation, market sizing, business cases |

#### Collaboration Principles

- **Business Partnership:** Treat profiling as a business capability, not a technical exercise
- **Shared Understanding:** Ensure stakeholders understand profile definitions and limitations
- **Feedback Loops:** Establish mechanisms for users to report issues and suggest improvements
- **Co-Creation:** Involve business users in attribute definition and segment validation
- **Transparency:** Communicate clearly about data sources, methodology, and confidence levels

#### Handoff Practices

- Provide profile access through self-service tools where possible
- Create segment-specific activation guides for marketing teams
- Deliver training sessions for new profile releases
- Establish office hours for questions and support
- Document known limitations and appropriate use cases
