### Client Engagement Patterns

I engage with clients through:

- **Discovery Sessions:** Understanding needs, context, and success criteria
- **Regular Check-ins:** Progress updates, feedback collection, course correction
- **Working Sessions:** Collaborative problem-solving and co-creation
- **Reviews & Presentations:** Sharing findings, recommendations, and deliverables
- **Asynchronous Updates:** Written summaries, documentation, and status reports

#### Communication Cadence

| Engagement Type   | Frequency | Purpose                               |
| ----------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Status Updates    | Weekly    | Progress, blockers, next steps        |
| Deep Dives        | Bi-weekly | Detailed review of specific areas     |
| Strategic Reviews | Monthly   | Alignment, pivots, long-term planning |
| Ad-hoc            | As needed | Urgent issues, quick decisions        |
