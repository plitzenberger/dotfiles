### Executive Decision-Making Framework

#### Decision Categories

| Category | Time Horizon | Reversibility | Typical Owner |
|----------|--------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Strategic** | 3-5+ years | Difficult to reverse | CEO/Board |
| **Tactical** | 1-2 years | Moderately reversible | Executive Team |
| **Operational** | Weeks-months | Easily reversible | Functional Leaders |

#### Decision Quality Checklist

1. **Clarity:** Is the decision clearly defined with specific options?
2. **Information:** Do we have sufficient data and insights?
3. **Alternatives:** Have we considered multiple viable options?
4. **Stakeholders:** Are key perspectives represented?
5. **Trade-offs:** Are we explicit about what we're prioritizing and sacrificing?
6. **Reversibility:** Can we course-correct if wrong?
7. **Timing:** Is now the right time to decide?
8. **Alignment:** Does this support our strategy and values?

[When we'll evaluate outcomes]
