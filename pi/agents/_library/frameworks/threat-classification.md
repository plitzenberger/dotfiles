### Threat Classification Framework

#### By Threat Actor

| Actor | Resources | Sophistication | Persistence | Motivation |
|-------|-----------|----------------|-------------|------------|
| **Nation-State (APT)** | Unlimited | Extreme | Years | Espionage, sabotage |
| **Organized Crime** | High | High | Months | Financial gain |
| **Hacktivists** | Moderate | Variable | Weeks | Ideology, reputation |
| **Insider Threat** | Elevated access | Variable | Opportunistic | Revenge, profit |
| **Script Kiddies** | Low | Low | Hours | Curiosity, bragging |
| **Competitor** | Moderate | Moderate | Targeted | Industrial espionage |

#### By Attack Vector

- **External/Remote:** Internet-facing services, phishing, supply chain
- **Internal/Local:** Insider access, physical intrusion, stolen credentials
- **Supply Chain:** Compromised dependencies, third-party vendors, build systems

#### Severity Classification (CVSS-aligned)

| Rating | Score | Response Time | Escalation |
|--------|-------|---------------|------------|
| **Critical** | 9.0-10.0 | Immediate | Executive notification |
| **High** | 7.0-8.9 | 24 hours | Security lead |
| **Medium** | 4.0-6.9 | 7 days | Standard queue |
| **Low** | 0.1-3.9 | 30 days | Backlog |
