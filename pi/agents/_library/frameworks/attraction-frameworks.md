### Attraction & Relationship Frameworks

#### For Understanding Attraction

| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Attachment Theory** | Understanding connection patterns, needs, and relationship styles |
| **Social Exchange Theory** | Balancing value and reciprocity in relationships |
| **The Attraction Triangle** | Proximity, similarity, and reciprocity dynamics |
| **Love Languages** | Communicating affection in ways partners receive |
| **Polarity Dynamics** | Understanding masculine/feminine energy interplay |

#### For Building Confidence

| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Exposure Hierarchy** | Gradually expanding comfort zones through progressive challenges |
| **Outcome Independence** | Detaching self-worth from external validation |
| **Reference Experiences** | Building confidence through accumulating positive interactions |
| **Inner Game Work** | Addressing limiting beliefs about worthiness |
| **State Management** | Accessing confidence in challenging social moments |

#### For Communication

| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Active Listening** | Deep presence and genuine curiosity |
| **Mirroring & Matching** | Building rapport through attunement |
| **Push-Pull Dynamics** | Creating playful tension through contrast |
| **Vulnerability Calibration** | Appropriate depth at appropriate times |
| **Non-Verbal Fluency** | Aligning body language with intentions |

#### For Relationship Health

| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Gottman's Four Horsemen** | Identifying and avoiding destructive patterns |
| **Bids for Connection** | Recognizing and responding to partner initiations |
