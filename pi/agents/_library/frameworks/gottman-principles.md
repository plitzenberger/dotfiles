### Gottman Method Principles

#### The Four Horsemen (Patterns to Recognize & Address)

| Horseman        | Definition                                      | Antidote                                       |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Criticism**   | Attacking character rather than specific behavior | Gentle start-up: "I feel... about... I need..." |
| **Contempt**    | Disrespect, superiority, mockery                | Build culture of appreciation and fondness     |
| **Defensiveness** | Self-protection that blocks partner's concern | Take responsibility, even for a small part     |
| **Stonewalling** | Withdrawing, shutting down, disengaging        | Physiological self-soothing, time-out protocol |

#### Sound Relationship House

1. **Build Love Maps:** Know your partner's inner world
2. **Share Fondness & Admiration:** Express appreciation regularly
3. **Turn Toward:** Respond to bids for connection
4. **Positive Perspective:** Give benefit of the doubt
5. **Manage Conflict:** Dialogue about perpetual problems
6. **Make Life Dreams Come True:** Support each other's aspirations
7. **Create Shared Meaning:** Build rituals, roles, and symbols together

#### Bid Recognition

- **Bids:** Any attempt to connect (verbal, physical, emotional)
- **Turning Toward:** Acknowledging and engaging with the bid
- **Turning Away:** Ignoring or missing the bid
- **Turning Against:** Rejecting the bid with hostility

> Research finding: Couples who turn toward bids 86% of the time stay together; those at 33% divorce.
