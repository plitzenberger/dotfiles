### Persona Development Framework

#### Persona Architecture Layers

Build personas from observable facts to deep motivations:

| Layer | Focus | Questions |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| **Observable** | Demographics, behaviors, context | What can we see and measure? |
| **Functional** | Goals, tasks, pain points, needs | What are they trying to accomplish? |
| **Emotional** | Fears, aspirations, frustrations | How do they feel about it? |
| **Identity** | Values, beliefs, self-perception | Who do they see themselves as? |

#### Essential Persona Components

| Component | Description | Purpose |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Name & Photo** | Memorable, realistic identifier | Humanization and recall |
| **Role/Context** | Professional or life situation | Situational understanding |
| **Goals** | What they're trying to achieve | Direction and motivation |
| **Pain Points** | Current frustrations and obstacles | Problem identification |
| **Behaviors** | How they act, decide, and interact | Prediction and design |
| **Motivations** | Why they care (emotional drivers) | Persuasion and empathy |
| **Objections** | Barriers to your solution | Objection handling |
| **Influences** | Information sources and trusted voices | Channel and content strategy |
| **Quote** | Captures their voice and mindset | Voice and authenticity |

#### Jobs To Be Done Integration

Structure persona goals using JTBD:

> When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [expected outcome].

#### Empathy Mapping Framework

| Quadrant | Questions |
|----------|-----------|
| **Think & Feel** | What occupies their thinking? What matters to them? |
| **See** | What is their environment? What do they observe? |
| **Hear** | What do influencers and media tell them? |
| **Say & Do** | What is their public behavior? |
| **Pains** | What are their fears and frustrations? |
| **Gains** | What do they truly want to achieve? |

#### Persona Types by Specificity

| Type | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Proto-Persona** | Assumption-based, quick | Early-stage alignment |
| **Qualitative Persona** | Research-based, story-driven | Design and content |
| **Quantitative Persona** | Data-validated, segment-mapped | Marketing automation |
| **Composite Persona** | Multi-source blend | Enterprise alignment |
