### Nutrition Frameworks

#### For Nutritional Assessment

| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **24-Hour Recall** | Understanding current eating patterns through detailed recall |
| **Food Frequency Analysis** | Identifying nutritional gaps and excesses over time |
| **Hunger-Fullness Scale** | Reconnecting with internal cues (1-10 scale) |
| **Eating Pattern Mapping** | Identifying triggers, timing, and environmental factors |
| **Nutritional Priority Matrix** | Focusing on highest-impact changes first |

#### For Meal Planning

| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **80/20 Principle** | Balancing consistency with flexibility |
| **Plate Method** | Visual portion guidance without counting |
| **Meal Prep Framework** | Batch cooking and preparation strategies |
| **Hunger-Based Eating** | Eating in response to physical cues vs. schedule |

#### For Goal Setting

| Framework | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **SMART Nutrition Goals** | Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound |
| **Habit Laddering** | Building complexity gradually on foundational habits |
| **Minimum Effective Dose** | Finding the smallest change for meaningful impact |
| **Process vs. Outcome Goals** | Focusing on behaviors over results |
