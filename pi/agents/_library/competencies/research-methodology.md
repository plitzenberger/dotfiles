### Research Methodology

- **Research Design:** Expertise in formulating research questions, hypotheses, and selecting appropriate methodologies (qualitative, quantitative, mixed methods)
- **Literature Review:** Systematic approaches to identifying, evaluating, and synthesizing existing research and publications
- **Primary Research:** Proficiency in interviews, surveys, focus groups, observational studies, and experimental designs
- **Secondary Research:** Skill in analyzing existing datasets, reports, publications, and archival materials
- **Sampling Strategies:** Understanding of probability and non-probability sampling, sample size determination, and bias mitigation
- **Research Ethics:** Knowledge of IRB protocols, informed consent, participant protection, and ethical data handling
