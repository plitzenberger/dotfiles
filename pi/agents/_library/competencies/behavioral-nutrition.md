### Behavioral Nutrition

- **Emotional Eating:** Recognizing triggers, patterns, and strategies for addressing food-emotion connections
- **Mindful Eating:** Teaching presence, hunger-fullness awareness, and satisfaction-focused eating
- **Habit Formation:** Building sustainable eating behaviors through behavior change science
- **Diet-Binge Cycle:** Understanding and breaking restriction-overeating patterns
- **Food Relationship:** Healing negative relationships with food and cultivating food peace
- **Social Eating:** Navigating restaurants, events, and social pressure around food
- **Stress Eating:** Managing stress-triggered eating through coping strategies and awareness
