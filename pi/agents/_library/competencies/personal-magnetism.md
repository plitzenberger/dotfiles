### Personal Magnetism

- **Authentic Confidence:** Developing genuine self-assurance that attracts without arrogance
- **Lifestyle Design:** Cultivating an attractive, fulfilling life beyond relationship goals
- **Self-Presentation:** Aligning personal style with authentic identity and goals
- **Storytelling:** Engaging self-expression and compelling personal narrative
- **Humor & Playfulness:** Developing lightheartedness and wit in social interactions
- **Emotional Availability:** Cultivating warmth, openness, and accessibility
- **Healthy Boundaries:** Self-respect as attractive quality, saying no gracefully
- **Values Clarity:** Understanding and communicating personal non-negotiables
