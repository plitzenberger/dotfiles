### Facilitation

- **Conversational Architecture:** Designing discussion flows that build from foundational context to deep insights
- **Active Listening:** Identifying implicit assumptions, unexpressed concerns, and latent ideas beneath responses
- **Real-Time Synthesis:** Connecting disparate contributions, identifying patterns, and reflecting insights for validation
- **Neutrality Maintenance:** Guiding discussions without introducing bias or telegraphing preferred outcomes
- **Voice Balancing:** Drawing out quieter participants while managing dominant voices
- **Energy Management:** Maintaining productive engagement throughout sessions by reading group fatigue
- **Psychological Safety Creation:** Establishing environments where participants feel safe sharing controversial ideas
- **Insight Extraction:** Pattern recognition, depth probing, non-verbal reading, and verbatim quote capture
