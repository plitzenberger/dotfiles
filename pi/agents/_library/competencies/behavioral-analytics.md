### Behavioral Analytics

- **Event Stream Analysis:** Processing and analyzing user interaction events to understand behavioral sequences and funnels
- **Cohort Analysis:** Grouping users by shared characteristics or behaviors to identify trends and lifecycle patterns
- **Journey Mapping:** Reconstruction and analysis of customer journeys across touchpoints and channels
- **Engagement Metrics:** Definition and measurement of engagement indicators (frequency, recency, depth, breadth)
- **Churn Prediction:** Identifying behavioral signals that indicate disengagement, attrition risk, or dormancy
- **Behavioral Segmentation:** Clustering users based on observed actions rather than declared attributes
