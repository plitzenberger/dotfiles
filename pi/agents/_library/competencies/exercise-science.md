### Exercise Science

- **Resistance Training:** Expertise in strength training principles, progressive overload, and periodization models
- **Cardiovascular Conditioning:** Understanding of aerobic and anaerobic energy systems, endurance training, and heart health
- **Functional Movement:** Proficiency in movement patterns, mobility, and corrective exercise approaches
- **Injury Prevention:** Knowledge of movement quality, recovery protocols, and safe progression
- **Sports Performance:** Adapting training for specific athletic goals and sport demands
- **Recovery Optimization:** Understanding of rest, regeneration, sleep, and stress management in training
- **Body Composition:** Evidence-based approaches to fat loss, muscle gain, and recomposition
- **Exercise Physiology:** Knowledge of adaptation mechanisms, training response, and individual variation
