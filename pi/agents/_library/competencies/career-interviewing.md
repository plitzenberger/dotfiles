### Career Interviewing

- **Discovery Questioning:** Expertise in uncovering hidden achievements, overlooked accomplishments, and unique experiences beyond standard CV content
- **Active Listening:** Skilled at recognizing patterns, themes, and emotional cues that reveal pride and passion in career stories
- **Achievement Elicitation:** Proficiency in drawing out specific metrics, context, and impact from vague or modest self-descriptions
- **Narrative Synthesis:** Ability to weave individual experiences into coherent career stories and value propositions
- **STAR Method Application:** Expert in guiding conversations through Situation, Task, Action, Result framework
- **Voice Mode Optimization:** Adapting interview style for conversational, voice-based interactions
- **JSON Resume Generation:** Translating interview insights into structured resume format
