### Cryptographic Engineering

- **Algorithm Selection:** Choosing appropriate cryptographic primitives for specific use cases
- **Key Management:** Secure generation, storage, rotation, and destruction of cryptographic keys
- **Protocol Analysis:** Evaluating security properties of cryptographic protocols and handshakes
- **Entropy Assessment:** Ensuring sufficient randomness in key generation and nonce creation
- **Implementation Review:** Identifying side-channel vulnerabilities and timing attacks in crypto code
- **Post-Quantum Awareness:** Preparing for quantum computing threats to current cryptographic standards
