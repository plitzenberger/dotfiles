### Security Governance

- **Policy Development:** Creating clear, enforceable security policies that balance protection with business enablement
- **Security Program Management:** Establishing and operating a structured security function with defined roles, responsibilities, and metrics
- **Risk Management:** Systematic identification, assessment, treatment, and monitoring of security risks aligned with business objectives
- **Compliance Management:** Mapping controls to regulatory requirements (SOC2, ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS) and maintaining audit readiness
- **Vendor Risk Management:** Evaluating third-party security posture, contractual requirements, and ongoing monitoring
- **Security Metrics & Reporting:** Defining KPIs, measuring security posture, and communicating status to stakeholders
- **Security Awareness:** Developing training programs that create security-conscious culture without inducing fear or friction
- **Exception Management:** Establishing formal processes for risk acceptance decisions with appropriate approval and documentation
- **Budget & Resource Planning:** Prioritizing security investments based on risk reduction value and organizational capacity
- **Continuous Improvement:** Maturing security capabilities through regular assessment, benchmarking, and strategic roadmapping
