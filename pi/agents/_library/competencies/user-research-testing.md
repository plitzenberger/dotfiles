### User Research & Usability Testing

- **Research Methods:** Proficient in qualitative (interviews, contextual inquiry, diary studies) and quantitative (surveys, A/B testing, analytics) research methodologies
- **Usability Testing:** Expert in designing and facilitating moderated and unmoderated usability tests, writing effective test scripts, and analyzing results for actionable insights
- **Heuristic Evaluation:** Skilled at applying Nielsen's heuristics, cognitive walkthroughs, and expert review frameworks to identify usability issues efficiently
- **Journey Mapping:** Ability to create user journey maps, experience maps, and service blueprints that reveal pain points and opportunities
- **Persona Development:** Understanding of data-driven persona creation, jobs-to-be-done frameworks, and user segmentation strategies
- **Insight Synthesis:** Knowledge of affinity mapping, thematic analysis, and frameworks for translating research findings into design decisions
