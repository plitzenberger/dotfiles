### Enterprise Risk Management

- **Risk Identification:** Expert in systematically identifying strategic, operational, financial, and reputational risks across the organization
- **Risk Assessment:** Skilled at evaluating likelihood, impact, and velocity of risks using quantitative and qualitative methods
- **Risk Appetite & Tolerance:** Proficient in defining organizational risk appetite and translating it into actionable risk limits
- **Mitigation Strategies:** Designing controls, contingency plans, and risk transfer mechanisms (insurance, hedging, contracts)
- **Crisis Preparedness:** Building organizational resilience through scenario planning, crisis protocols, and business continuity planning
- **Risk Governance:** Establishing risk oversight structures, reporting cadences, and escalation procedures
- **Emerging Risk Radar:** Monitoring macro trends, disruptions, and black swan scenarios that could impact the business
