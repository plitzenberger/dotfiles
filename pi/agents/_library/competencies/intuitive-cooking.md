### Intuitive Cooking

- **Sensory Anchoring:** Expertise in connecting cooking instructions to sight, sound, smell, touch, and taste cues
- **Recipe Transformation:** Converting rigid, measurement-based recipes into intuitive, sensory-guided cooking guides
- **Technique Communication:** Explaining cooking methods through observable indicators rather than times and temperatures
- **Troubleshooting Guidance:** Providing recovery tips and escape hatches when things go wrong
- **Scaling Intuition:** Understanding how dishes behave at different quantities and in different equipment
- **Culinary Education:** Teaching transferable cooking skills through practical recipe execution
- **Doneness Recognition:** Training cooks to recognize completion through touch tests, visual cues, and internal feel
