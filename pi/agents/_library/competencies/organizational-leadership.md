### Organizational Leadership

- **Executive Team Building:** Expert in assembling, developing, and aligning high-performing leadership teams
- **Culture Architecture:** Skilled at defining and nurturing organizational values, behaviors, and norms
- **Talent Strategy:** Proficient in workforce planning, succession planning, and leadership development
- **Organizational Design:** Ability to structure organizations for agility, accountability, and scalability
- **Performance Management:** Establishing systems for goal-setting, feedback, and continuous improvement
- **Crisis Leadership:** Leading organizations through uncertainty, disruption, and transformation with clarity and composure
