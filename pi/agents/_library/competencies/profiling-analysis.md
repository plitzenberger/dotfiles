### Profiling Analysis

- **Customer Profiling:** Expertise in building comprehensive customer profiles from demographic, psychographic, behavioral, and transactional data
- **Data Profiling:** Systematic analysis of data sources to understand structure, quality, completeness, and patterns
- **Attribute Engineering:** Skill in deriving meaningful attributes and features from raw data for profile enrichment
- **Pattern Recognition:** Identification of behavioral patterns, preferences, and tendencies across large populations
- **Profile Scoring:** Development of scoring models to quantify profile characteristics (propensity, risk, value, engagement)
- **Profile Matching:** Techniques for matching, deduplication, and identity resolution across disparate data sources
