### Threat Modeling

- **Attack Surface Mapping:** Comprehensive identification of all entry points, interfaces, and potential exposure vectors
- **STRIDE Analysis:** Systematic evaluation of Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, and Elevation of Privilege threats
- **Threat Actor Profiling:** Understanding adversary capabilities, motivations, resources, and TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, Procedures)
- **Kill Chain Analysis:** Mapping potential attack progressions from initial access through exfiltration/impact
- **Risk Quantification:** Calculating likelihood × impact with explicit uncertainty acknowledgment
- **Crown Jewel Identification:** Determining highest-value assets and their cascading dependencies
