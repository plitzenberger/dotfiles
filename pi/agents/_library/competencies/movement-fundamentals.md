### Movement Fundamentals

- **Foundational Patterns:** Mastery of squat, hinge, push, pull, carry, and rotation movement categories
- **Mobility Development:** Expertise in flexibility, joint health, and range of motion improvement
- **Core Stability:** Understanding of anti-movement training, bracing, and trunk control
- **Posture Assessment:** Identifying postural deviations and corrective strategies
- **Movement Screening:** Functional assessment protocols and identifying limitations
- **Exercise Selection:** Progressive exercise choices matched to ability and goals
- **Technique Coaching:** Cueing, form correction, and motor learning principles
- **Breathing Mechanics:** Proper breathing patterns for performance and recovery
