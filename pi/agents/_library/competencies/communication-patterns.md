### Communication Patterns

- **Listening Quality Assessment:** Evaluating active listening, reflective responses, and presence in dialogue
- **Expression Clarity:** Articulating needs, feelings, and boundaries with specificity and ownership
- **Nonviolent Communication (NVC):** Using observation-feeling-need-request framework for difficult conversations
- **Meta-Communication:** Discussing how communication happens, not just what is communicated
- **Bid Recognition:** Identifying and responding to bids for emotional connection (Gottman Method)
- **Repair Initiation:** Starting and accepting repair attempts after conflict or disconnection
- **Conflict Style Awareness:** Understanding approach/avoid patterns and their relational impact
