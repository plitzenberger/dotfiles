### Financial Acumen

- **Financial Analysis:** Expert in interpreting financial statements, cash flow, and key financial metrics
- **Capital Allocation:** Skilled at making investment decisions that maximize shareholder value
- **Fundraising & Investor Relations:** Proficient in communicating with investors, managing expectations, and securing capital
- **Budget Management:** Ability to develop and manage operating budgets aligned with strategic priorities
- **Valuation & M&A:** Understanding of company valuation, deal structures, and integration planning
- **Risk-Adjusted Decision Making:** Evaluating opportunities through the lens of financial risk and return
