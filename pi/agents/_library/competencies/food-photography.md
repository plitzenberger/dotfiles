# Food Photography

Professional food photography expertise encompassing technical camera skills, artistic composition, food styling, and visual storytelling to create compelling images that evoke appetite and emotion.

## Core Capabilities

- **Technical Mastery:** Expert in camera settings, lens selection, depth of field control, and exposure optimization for food subjects
- **Lighting Design:** Proficient in natural and artificial lighting techniques, light modifiers, reflectors, and shadow control specific to food photography
- **Composition & Styling:** Skilled at food arrangement, prop selection, surface choices, and compositional frameworks that highlight culinary subjects
- **Color Science:** Deep understanding of color theory, white balance, color grading, and how colors influence appetite appeal
- **Post-Processing:** Mastery of professional editing workflows, retouching techniques, and color correction for food imagery

## Key Patterns

### The Hero Shot Framework

Establishing the primary image that sells a dish:

- **Angle Selection:** Overhead (flat lay), 45-degree (three-quarter), or straight-on based on dish characteristics
- **Focus Point:** Sharp focus on the most appetizing element, with intentional blur for depth
- **Light Direction:** Side lighting to reveal texture, backlighting for steam and translucency
- **Negative Space:** Strategic empty areas for text overlay or visual breathing room

### Texture Revelation Technique

Making food look touchable and real:

- **Crispy foods:** Raking light from the side to show surface irregularities
- **Glossy foods:** Carefully placed highlights with controlled reflections
- **Soft foods:** Diffused lighting to avoid harsh shadows that flatten texture
- **Steaming foods:** Backlight or strong sidelight with dark background

### Color Psychology in Food

Understanding appetite appeal:

- **Warm colors (red, orange, yellow):** Stimulate appetite, convey freshness
- **Cool colors (blue, purple):** Used sparingly; can suppress appetite but add visual interest
- **Neutral backdrops:** Let food colors pop without competition
- **Color harmony:** Complementary props that enhance without distracting

### The Moment of Truth

Capturing food at its peak visual appeal:

- **Ice cream:** Work in cold environments, use stand-ins during setup
- **Hot dishes:** Capture steam within first 2-3 minutes, have backups ready
- **Fresh produce:** Water misting for freshness illusion, work quickly
- **Sauces and drizzles:** Multiple takes with consistent pouring technique
