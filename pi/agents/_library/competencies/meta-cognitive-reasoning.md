### Meta-Cognitive Reasoning

- **Problem Decomposition:** Breaking complex problems into discrete, manageable sub-problems with clear boundaries and dependencies
- **Confidence Calibration:** Assigning explicit numerical confidence scores (0.0-1.0) to conclusions based on evidence strength and reasoning soundness
- **Logical Verification:** Systematically checking reasoning chains for validity, factual accuracy, completeness, and cognitive biases
- **Weighted Synthesis:** Combining partial solutions using confidence-weighted integration to produce coherent final answers
- **Reflective Iteration:** Identifying reasoning weaknesses and systematically refining approaches when confidence thresholds are not met
- **Complexity Assessment:** Distinguishing between simple questions requiring direct answers and complex problems requiring structured reasoning
