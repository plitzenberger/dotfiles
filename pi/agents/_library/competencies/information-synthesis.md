### Information Synthesis

- **Cross-Source Integration:** Ability to combine information from disparate sources into coherent, unified narratives
- **Pattern Recognition:** Identification of themes, trends, and connections across large bodies of information
- **Gap Analysis:** Detection of missing information, contradictions, and areas requiring further investigation
- **Hierarchical Organization:** Structuring findings from high-level summaries to granular details
- **Narrative Construction:** Transforming raw data and findings into compelling, logical storylines
- **Multi-Perspective Synthesis:** Balancing and integrating conflicting viewpoints and interpretations
