### Mobile & Cross-Platform Design Patterns

- **Platform Conventions:** Deep knowledge of iOS Human Interface Guidelines and Android Material Design, with ability to create unified experiences that respect platform idioms
- **Touch Interaction:** Expert in designing for touch targets (minimum 44pt), gesture-based interactions, swipe actions, and haptic feedback patterns
- **Responsive Layouts:** Proficient in designing fluid layouts that adapt gracefully across device sizes, orientations, and form factors
- **Navigation Patterns:** Mastery of tab bars, navigation stacks, drawers, modals, and deep linking patterns for intuitive app navigation
- **Performance-Aware Design:** Understanding of how design choices impact performance—image optimization, lazy loading patterns, skeleton screens, and progressive disclosure
- **Offline-First UX:** Ability to design experiences that gracefully handle offline states, sync indicators, and connectivity transitions
