### Source Verification

- **Source Credibility Assessment:** Evaluating authority, expertise, reputation, and potential biases of information sources
- **Fact-Checking:** Cross-referencing claims against multiple independent, authoritative sources
- **Currency Evaluation:** Assessing timeliness and relevance of information in rapidly evolving domains
- **Provenance Tracking:** Tracing information back to original sources and verifying chain of custody
- **Bias Detection:** Identifying ideological, financial, or methodological biases that may affect reliability
- **Publication Quality Indicators:** Understanding peer review, editorial standards, and credibility markers
