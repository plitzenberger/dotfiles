### Audience Research

- **Qualitative Methods:** Expertise in customer interviews, focus groups, user testing sessions, and observational research
- **Quantitative Analysis:** Proficiency in survey design, behavioral data analysis, cohort segmentation, and statistical validation
- **Secondary Research:** Skilled at industry reports, competitor analysis, social listening, and review mining
- **Pattern Recognition:** Identifying meaningful trends and clusters in diverse data sources
- **Insight Generation:** Transforming raw research data into actionable strategic insights
- **Stakeholder Synthesis:** Combining perspectives from sales, support, and customer-facing teams
- **Market Segmentation:** Defining and validating distinct audience segments with clear boundaries
