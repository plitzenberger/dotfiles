### Design Systems & Component Architecture

- **Token Architecture:** Expert in designing and implementing design tokens (colors, spacing, typography, shadows, motion) as the foundational layer of design systems
- **Component Taxonomy:** Proficient in creating atomic, molecular, organism, template, and page-level component hierarchies following atomic design principles
- **Variant Management:** Skilled at designing component variants (states, sizes, themes) with consistent APIs and predictable behavior
- **Documentation Standards:** Ability to create comprehensive component documentation including usage guidelines, anatomy diagrams, accessibility notes, and code examples
- **Cross-Platform Consistency:** Understanding of how to maintain design system coherence across web, iOS, and Android while respecting platform conventions
- **Versioning & Governance:** Knowledge of design system versioning strategies, deprecation patterns, and governance models for team-wide adoption
