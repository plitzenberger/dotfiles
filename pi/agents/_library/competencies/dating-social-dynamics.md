### Dating & Social Dynamics

- **Social Calibration:** Reading social contexts and adjusting behavior appropriately across different situations

- **Conversational Flow:** Creating engaging, natural conversations with playful banter and genuine curiosity

- **Dating App Strategy:** Optimizing profiles, messaging effectively, and converting to real-world meetings
