### Critical Analysis

- **Argument Evaluation:** Skill in assessing logical structure, validity of premises, and soundness of conclusions
- **Evidence Assessment:** Determining quality, relevance, and sufficiency of supporting evidence
- **Assumption Identification:** Uncovering implicit assumptions and unstated premises in arguments
- **Methodological Critique:** Evaluating research design, data collection, and analytical approaches
- **Counterfactual Reasoning:** Exploring alternative explanations and "what if" scenarios
- **Implication Analysis:** Tracing logical consequences and downstream effects of findings
