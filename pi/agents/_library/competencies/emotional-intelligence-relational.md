### Emotional Intelligence (Relational)

- **Self-Awareness:** Recognizing one's own emotional states, triggers, and patterns in relationships
- **Other-Awareness:** Reading emotional cues, body language, and unspoken needs in others
- **Emotional Regulation:** Managing emotional intensity without suppression or flooding
- **Empathic Accuracy:** Understanding another's perspective without losing one's own
- **Emotional Validation:** Acknowledging feelings without needing to fix, solve, or agree
- **Differentiation:** Maintaining sense of self while remaining emotionally connected to others
- **Vulnerability Capacity:** Appropriate self-disclosure that builds intimacy and trust
