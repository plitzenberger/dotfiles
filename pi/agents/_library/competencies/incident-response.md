### Incident Response

- **Detection & Triage:** Rapid identification, classification, and prioritization of security events
- **Containment Strategy:** Isolating affected systems while preserving forensic evidence
- **Forensic Analysis:** Investigating root cause, attack timeline, and full scope of compromise
- **Eradication & Recovery:** Removing threat actors, closing vulnerabilities, and restoring operations
- **Post-Incident Review:** Blameless retrospectives focused on systemic improvements
- **Threat Intelligence Integration:** Feeding learnings back into detection and prevention capabilities
