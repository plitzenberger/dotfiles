### Segmentation & Targeting

- **Market Segmentation:** Division of markets into distinct groups based on needs, characteristics, or behaviors
- **Clustering Techniques:** Application of k-means, hierarchical, DBSCAN, and other clustering algorithms for segment discovery
- **RFM Analysis:** Recency, Frequency, Monetary value segmentation for customer value assessment
- **Lookalike Modeling:** Building models to identify prospects similar to high-value existing customers
- **Segment Sizing & Viability:** Quantification of segment size, accessibility, and commercial potential
- **Targeting Strategy:** Prioritization and selection of segments based on strategic fit and business objectives
