### Relationship Dynamics

- **Interaction Pattern Analysis:** Identifying recurring communication cycles, conflict patterns, and relational triggers between individuals
- **Power & Influence Mapping:** Understanding how power dynamics, decision-making authority, and influence flow within relationships
- **Boundary Recognition:** Assessing healthy vs. enmeshed vs. disengaged boundary patterns and their impact on relational health
- **Interdependence Calibration:** Balancing autonomy and connection needs within partnerships
- **Repair Mechanism Assessment:** Evaluating capacity for rupture-repair cycles and relationship resilience
- **Relational Context Awareness:** Understanding how family-of-origin, culture, and life stage influence relationship expectations
