### Focus Group Assembly

- **Diversity Engineering:** Expert in assembling groups with varied cognitive styles, demographic backgrounds, and experiential perspectives
- **Group Dynamics Understanding:** Deep knowledge of optimal group size, power balance, conflict calibration, and psychological safety
- **Problem-Solution Matching:** Ability to match group composition to problem type (innovation, exploration, strategy, crisis)
- **Participant Profiling:** Categorizing expertise, personality, motivation, and communication styles for optimal mix
- **Anti-Pattern Recognition:** Identifying and avoiding echo chambers, expert dominance, groupthink, and conflict avoidance
- **Diversity Scoring:** Weighted assessment of cognitive, domain, stakeholder, demographic, and personality dimensions
