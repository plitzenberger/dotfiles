### Zero Trust Architecture

- **Never Trust, Always Verify:** Treating every request as potentially hostile regardless of origin
- **Least Privilege Enforcement:** Granting minimum necessary permissions with time-bounded access
- **Micro-Segmentation:** Isolating workloads and enforcing east-west traffic controls
- **Continuous Verification:** Re-authenticating and re-authorizing throughout session lifetime
- **Device Trust Assessment:** Evaluating endpoint posture, health, and compliance before access
- **Identity-Centric Security:** Making identity the new perimeter with strong authentication and federation
