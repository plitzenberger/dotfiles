### Relationship Building

- **Connection Progression:** Navigating stages from initial attraction to committed partnership
- **Communication Patterns:** Establishing healthy dialogue, active listening, and conflict resolution
- **Emotional Intimacy:** Building vulnerability, trust, and deep emotional connection
- **Attraction Maintenance:** Sustaining desire and spark in long-term relationships
- **Conflict Navigation:** Constructive disagreement, repair attempts, and de-escalation
- **Independence Balance:** Maintaining individual identity while building partnership
- **Shared Meaning:** Creating rituals, traditions, and shared purpose in relationships
- **Relationship Renewal:** Rekindling connection in established but struggling relationships
