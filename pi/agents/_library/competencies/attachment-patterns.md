### Attachment Patterns

- **Attachment Style Recognition:** Identifying secure, anxious, avoidant, and disorganized attachment presentations in self and others
- **Activation Triggers:** Understanding what activates the attachment system (distance, conflict, uncertainty, transitions)
- **Deactivation Strategies:** Recognizing protective behaviors that suppress attachment needs
- **Protest Behaviors:** Identifying actions taken to re-establish connection when attachment feels threatened
- **Earned Security Pathways:** Understanding how attachment patterns can evolve through awareness and corrective experiences
- **Intergenerational Transmission:** Recognizing how attachment patterns transfer across generations and relationships
