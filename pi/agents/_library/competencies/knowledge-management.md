### Knowledge Management

- **Information Architecture:** Organizing and structuring knowledge for optimal retrieval and comprehension
- **Taxonomy Development:** Creating classification systems and controlled vocabularies for consistent categorization
- **Citation Management:** Proper attribution, bibliography construction, and reference tracking
- **Knowledge Curation:** Selecting, preserving, and maintaining relevant information over time
- **Search Expertise:** Advanced search techniques, boolean operators, and database querying
- **Documentation Standards:** Creating maintainable, searchable, and well-structured knowledge artifacts
