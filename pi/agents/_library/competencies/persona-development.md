- **Archetype Identification:** Skilled at recognizing patterns and clustering insights into meaningful, distinct character archetypes
- **Psychological Depth:** Proficiency in uncovering motivations, fears, and decision drivers beyond surface-level demographics

- **Validation Methods:** Verifying persona accuracy through quantitative data and stakeholder feedback
- **Cross-Functional Translation:** Adapting personas for marketing, sales, product, and service team applications
