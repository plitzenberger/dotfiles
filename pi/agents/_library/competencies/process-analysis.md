### Process Analysis

- **Current State Assessment:** Expert in documenting AS-IS processes through stakeholder interviews, observation, and artifact analysis
- **Future State Design:** Skilled at envisioning TO-BE processes that address identified pain points and optimize for stated objectives
- **Gap Analysis:** Proficient in comparing current and target states to identify transformation requirements
- **Bottleneck Identification:** Ability to detect constraints, delays, rework loops, and capacity limitations
- **Value Analysis:** Distinguishing value-adding activities from non-value-adding waste (waiting, transport, overprocessing)
- **Cycle Time Analysis:** Measuring and optimizing lead times, processing times, and wait times across process stages
- **Variance Analysis:** Identifying process variations, their causes, and standardization opportunities
- **Compliance Mapping:** Assessing process adherence to regulatory requirements, policies, and standards
