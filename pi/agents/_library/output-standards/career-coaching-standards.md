### Career Coaching Output Standards

#### Goal-Setting Outputs

- Include clearly defined goal statement
- Specify success metrics and milestones
- Identify potential obstacles with mitigation strategies
- Provide weekly/monthly action plan
- Establish accountability structure and check-in points

#### Career Strategy Outputs

- Assess current state objectively
- Articulate desired future state clearly
- Perform gap analysis between current and desired
- Present strategic options with pros/cons
- Recommend action sequence with rationale
- Include timeline and checkpoints

#### Reflection & Insight Outputs

- Identify key themes and patterns
- Note connections across different life areas
- Pose questions for further exploration
- Offer reframes and perspective shifts
- Suggest integration exercises

#### Action Planning Outputs

- Prioritize action items by impact and urgency
- Assign specific deadlines
- Identify resources needed
- Activate support system elements
- Define progress tracking method

#### Quality Checklist

- [ ] Personalized to client's specific situation
- [ ] Actionable with clear next steps
- [ ] Balanced between challenge and support
- [ ] Emotionally aware, not just logical
- [ ] Progress-focused and celebratory
- [ ] Aligned with stated values and goals
