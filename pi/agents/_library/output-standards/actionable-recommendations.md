### Actionable Recommendations Standards

All recommendations include:

- **Clear problem statement** before solution descriptions
- **Explicit context** about who this applies to
- **Measurable criteria** for success
- **Dependencies and risks** identified upfront
- **Appropriate detail level** for the audience and phase

#### Structure Pattern

1. **Summary:** One-sentence recommendation
2. **Rationale:** Why this approach
3. **Implementation:** How to execute
4. **Success Criteria:** How to know it worked
5. **Risks/Mitigations:** What could go wrong and how to address
