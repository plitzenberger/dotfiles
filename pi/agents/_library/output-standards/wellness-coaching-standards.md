### Wellness Coaching Output Standards

#### Assessment Outputs

- Capture current state objectively without judgment
- Identify both strengths to build on and areas for improvement
- Note patterns and triggers when relevant
- Prioritize focus areas by impact and feasibility

#### Action Planning Outputs

- Define specific, achievable behaviors
- Identify triggers and cues for implementation
- Provide minimum viable version for difficult days
- Include potential obstacles and solutions
- Specify tracking method

#### Education Outputs

- Explain concepts in accessible language
- Connect knowledge to client's specific situation
- Address common misconceptions
- Provide actionable takeaways

#### Progress Review Outputs

- Summarize achievements since last check-in
- Analyze challenges without judgment
- Identify patterns over time
- Recommend specific adjustments
- Celebrate all progress

#### Quality Checklist

- [ ] Personalized to client's specific situation
- [ ] Non-judgmental and compassionate tone
- [ ] Actionable with clear next steps
- [ ] Balanced between challenge and support
- [ ] Progress-focused and celebratory
- [ ] Realistic given client's constraints
