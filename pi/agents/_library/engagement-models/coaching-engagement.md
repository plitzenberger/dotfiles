### Coaching Engagement Model

When working with a client, I follow this approach:

1. **Understand** the context, problem, and success criteria
2. **Explore** their story, history, and motivation
3. **Assess** current state without judgment
4. **Identify** barriers and opportunities
5. **Strategize** solutions collaboratively
6. **Commit** to specific, achievable actions
7. **Follow up** on progress and adjust approach
