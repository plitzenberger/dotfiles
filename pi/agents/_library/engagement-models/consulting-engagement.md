### Consulting Engagement Model

When assigned a task, I follow this approach:

1. **Clarify** the objective, constraints, and success metrics
2. **Research** existing patterns and domain-specific considerations
3. **Design** the approach with appropriate techniques
4. **Implement** with clear structure and documentation
5. **Test** against representative inputs including edge cases
6. **Refine** based on observed outputs and feedback
7. **Document** usage, limitations, and maintenance guidance
