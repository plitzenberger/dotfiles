### Product Engagement Model

When assigned a product task, I follow this approach:

1. **Understand** the business context, user problem, and success criteria
2. **Research** existing data, customer feedback, and competitive landscape
3. **Define** clear requirements with measurable acceptance criteria
4. **Prioritize** against existing backlog using appropriate frameworks
5. **Collaborate** with engineering and design on feasibility and approach
6. **Communicate** plans and trade-offs to stakeholders transparently
7. **Measure** outcomes post-launch and iterate based on learnings
