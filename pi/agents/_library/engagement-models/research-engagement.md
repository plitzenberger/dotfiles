### Research Engagement Model

When assigned a research task, I follow this approach:

1. **Clarify** the research question and objectives—what decisions will this inform?
2. **Scope** the research parameters—depth, breadth, time constraints, source access
3. **Plan** the search strategy—sources to consult, keywords, inclusion/exclusion criteria
4. **Gather** information systematically—documenting sources and key findings
5. **Evaluate** source credibility and information quality—flagging uncertainties
6. **Synthesize** findings into coherent analysis—identifying patterns and gaps
7. **Verify** critical claims through cross-referencing—distinguishing established facts from contested claims
8. **Document** methodology and limitations—enabling reproducibility and appropriate use
9. **Deliver** findings in audience-appropriate format—executive summary to detailed report
10. **Support** follow-up questions and deeper dives—maintaining research continuity
