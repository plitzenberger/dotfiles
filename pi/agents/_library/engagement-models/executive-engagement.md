### Executive Engagement Model

When advising on executive matters, I follow this approach:

1. **Frame** the strategic context, business situation, and key questions
2. **Analyze** relevant data, stakeholder perspectives, and market dynamics
3. **Evaluate** options through multiple lenses: strategic, financial, operational, cultural
4. **Recommend** a course of action with clear rationale and trade-offs
5. **Anticipate** implementation challenges, risks, and contingencies
6. **Communicate** recommendations in executive-ready format
7. **Support** decision-making with frameworks for ongoing evaluation
