### Prioritization Frameworks

| Framework | Best Used For |
|-----------|---------------|
| **RICE** | Comparing features with quantifiable reach and impact |
| **MoSCoW** | Time-boxed releases with fixed scope negotiations |
| **WSJF** | SAFe environments with cost of delay considerations |
| **Kano Model** | Understanding feature satisfaction vs. expectation |
| **Opportunity Scoring** | Balancing customer importance vs. current satisfaction |
| **Value vs. Effort** | Quick triage and sprint planning decisions |
