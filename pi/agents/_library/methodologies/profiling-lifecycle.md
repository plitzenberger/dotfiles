### Profiling Lifecycle

A systematic approach to profiling projects from inception to operational deployment:

#### Phase 1: Discovery & Scoping

- Define profiling objectives and business questions
- Identify stakeholders and use cases for profiles
- Inventory available data sources and access constraints
- Establish success criteria and measurement approach
- Assess privacy, consent, and compliance requirements

#### Phase 2: Data Assessment

- Profile data sources for quality, coverage, and gaps
- Evaluate attribute relevance and predictive power
- Identify identity keys and linkage opportunities
- Document data limitations and constraints
- Recommend data acquisition or enrichment needs

#### Phase 3: Profile Design

- Define profile schema and attribute taxonomy
- Design derived attributes and scoring models
- Establish segmentation strategy and criteria
- Plan for profile versioning and evolution
- Create technical specifications for implementation

#### Phase 4: Implementation

- Build data pipelines for profile construction
- Implement feature engineering and transformations
- Develop scoring models and segmentation logic
- Create validation rules and quality checks
- Document code, logic, and business rules

#### Phase 5: Validation & Testing

- Validate profiles against known truths and samples
- Test segment distributions and stability
- Verify scoring model performance and calibration
- Conduct stakeholder review and acceptance testing
- Iterate based on feedback and findings

#### Phase 6: Activation & Operations

- Deploy profiles to production systems
- Integrate with downstream applications (CRM, marketing, analytics)
- Establish refresh schedules and monitoring
- Train users on profile interpretation and usage
- Create operational runbooks and support processes

#### Phase 7: Optimization & Evolution

- Monitor profile usage and business impact
- Analyze segment migration and profile drift
- Refine attributes and models based on outcomes
- Incorporate new data sources and signals
- Sunset deprecated attributes and segments
