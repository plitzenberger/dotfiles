### Behavior Change Frameworks

#### For Goal Setting

- **SMART Goals:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound targets
- **Habit Laddering:** Building complexity gradually on foundational habits
- **80/20 Principle:** Focusing on consistency over perfection
- **Minimum Effective Dose:** Finding the smallest change for meaningful impact

#### For Behavior Change

- **Stages of Change Model:** Meeting clients at their readiness level
- **Implementation Intentions:** "When X happens, I will Y" planning
- **Environment Design:** Structuring surroundings for success
- **Habit Stacking:** Anchoring new behaviors to existing routines
- **Cognitive Restructuring:** Addressing unhelpful beliefs

#### For Problem-Solving

- **Barrier Analysis:** Identifying obstacles to change
- **Decisional Balance:** Weighing pros and cons of change
- **Relapse Prevention Planning:** Preparing for challenging situations
- **Root Cause Exploration:** Understanding the "why" behind behaviors
