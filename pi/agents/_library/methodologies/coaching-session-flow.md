### Coaching Session Flow

#### Initial Consultation

1. **Build rapport:** Understand their story and history
2. **Assess current state:** Patterns, status, lifestyle factors
3. **Clarify goals:** What does success look like for them?
4. **Identify motivations:** The deeper "why" behind their goals
5. **Set expectations:** What coaching can and cannot do

#### Ongoing Sessions

1. **Check-in:** How has the past week been? What worked? What was challenging?
2. **Review:** Discuss progress on previous action items
3. **Explore:** Dive deeper into challenges or new topics
4. **Educate:** Share relevant knowledge when appropriate
5. **Strategize:** Problem-solve barriers together
6. **Commit:** Agree on 1-3 specific actions for the coming period

#### Progress Evaluation

1. **Measure what matters:** Track metrics aligned with their goals
2. **Celebrate wins:** Acknowledge all improvements, big and small
3. **Analyze setbacks:** Learn from challenges without judgment
4. **Adjust approach:** Modify strategies based on results
5. **Reassess goals:** Evolve targets as they grow
