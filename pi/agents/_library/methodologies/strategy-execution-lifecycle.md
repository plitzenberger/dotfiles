### Strategy Execution Lifecycle

A systematic approach to translating strategic intent into organizational results.

#### Phase 1: Strategy Formulation

| Activity | Output |
|----------|--------|
| Environmental scanning | Market analysis, competitive intelligence |
| Capability assessment | Strengths, gaps, and investment needs |
| Strategic choice | Vision, mission, and strategic priorities |
| Goal setting | 3-5 year objectives with success metrics |

#### Phase 2: Strategy Translation

| Activity | Output |
|----------|--------|
| Initiative design | Strategic initiatives with scope and ownership |
| Resource allocation | Budget, headcount, and capital assignments |
| OKR cascade | Objectives and Key Results across all levels |
| Milestone planning | Quarterly and annual checkpoints |

#### Phase 3: Operational Alignment

| Activity | Output |
|----------|--------|
| Organizational design | Structure aligned to strategic priorities |
| Process alignment | Workflows and systems supporting strategy |
| Talent alignment | Roles, skills, and incentives aligned to goals |
| Communication rollout | Strategy messaging to all stakeholders |

#### Phase 4: Execution Management

| Activity | Output |
|----------|--------|
| Performance tracking | Dashboards, scorecards, leading indicators |
| Cadence management | Weekly, monthly, quarterly review rhythms |
| Issue resolution | Blockers identified and escalated |
| Course correction | Tactical adjustments based on feedback |

#### Phase 5: Review & Renewal

| Activity | Output |
|----------|--------|
| Outcome assessment | Results vs. objectives, lessons learned |
| Strategy refresh | Adjustments based on market changes |
| Capability building | Investments in long-term competitiveness |
| Continuous improvement | Process refinements for next cycle |

---

#### Strategy-Execution Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Symptom | Remedy |
|--------------|---------|--------|
| **Strategy-Execution Gap** | Great plans, poor results | Translate strategy into specific actions with clear owners |
| **Initiative Overload** | Too many priorities | Focus on 3-5 critical initiatives, deprioritize the rest |
| **Measurement Void** | No visibility into progress | Establish leading indicators and review cadence |
| **Alignment Drift** | Teams working at cross-purposes | Cascade OKRs and reinforce through communication |
| **Static Strategy** | Strategy outdated by market shifts | Build in quarterly strategy reviews and adaptation |
