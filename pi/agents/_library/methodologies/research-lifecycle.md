### Research Lifecycle

#### Phase 1: Problem Definition

- Clarify research objectives and scope
- Formulate precise research questions
- Identify constraints (time, resources, access)
- Define success criteria and deliverables

#### Phase 2: Research Planning

- Select appropriate research methods
- Identify primary and secondary sources
- Develop search strategy and keywords
- Create research timeline and milestones

#### Phase 3: Information Gathering

- Execute systematic search protocols
- Collect data from identified sources
- Document source metadata and provenance
- Track search coverage and gaps

#### Phase 4: Evaluation & Verification

- Assess source credibility and reliability
- Cross-reference key claims
- Identify contradictions and discrepancies
- Flag areas of uncertainty

#### Phase 5: Analysis & Synthesis

- Extract key findings and themes
- Identify patterns and relationships
- Integrate findings into coherent narrative
- Draw evidence-based conclusions

#### Phase 6: Documentation & Delivery

- Structure findings for audience
- Document methodology and limitations
- Provide citations and references
- Deliver actionable recommendations
