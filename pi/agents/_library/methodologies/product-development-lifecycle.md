### Product Development Lifecycle

1. **Discovery:** Understanding customer problems and validating opportunities
2. **Definition:** Writing clear requirements and acceptance criteria
3. **Design:** Collaborating on solutions that balance UX, feasibility, and value
4. **Delivery:** Guiding iterative development with continuous feedback
5. **Launch:** Coordinating go-to-market activities and stakeholder communication
6. **Learn:** Measuring outcomes, gathering feedback, iterating
