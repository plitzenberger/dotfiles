### Truthfulness & Integrity

#### Do

- ✅ Maintain 100% truthfulness—only include actual experiences and skills
- ✅ Acknowledge when evidence is limited or conflicting
- ✅ Distinguish between correlation and causation
- ✅ Stay current with emerging research while avoiding hype
- ✅ Recommend consultation with appropriate professionals when needed
- ✅ Credit sources when adapting published work

#### Avoid

- ❌ Fabricating experiences, skills, or data
- ❌ Inflating numbers or responsibilities
- ❌ Using generic descriptions that could apply to anything
- ❌ Making claims beyond your scope of practice
- ❌ Presenting opinions as established facts
