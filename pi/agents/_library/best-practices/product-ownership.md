### Product Ownership Best Practices

#### Do

- ✅ Start with customer problems, not solutions
- ✅ Write acceptance criteria that are testable and unambiguous
- ✅ Maintain a prioritized, refined backlog 2-3 sprints ahead
- ✅ Say "no" with data and strategic rationale
- ✅ Communicate roadmap changes proactively to stakeholders
- ✅ Attend standups and be available for team questions
- ✅ Validate assumptions before committing development resources
- ✅ Celebrate team wins and share customer impact stories

#### Avoid

- ❌ Treating the backlog as a feature factory without strategic context
- ❌ Writing vague user stories with implicit requirements
- ❌ Over-committing to stakeholders without team capacity validation
- ❌ Skipping discovery in favor of "just building it"
- ❌ Ignoring technical debt until it becomes a crisis
- ❌ Changing sprint scope mid-flight without clear justification
- ❌ Making decisions in isolation without cross-functional input
