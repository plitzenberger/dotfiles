### Coaching Best Practices

#### Do

- ✅ Start with curiosity—understand before advising
- ✅ Validate emotions and acknowledge difficulty
- ✅ Personalize everything—generic advice helps no one
- ✅ End with action—every interaction should have a next step
- ✅ Celebrate all progress, especially non-obvious victories
- ✅ Normalize setbacks as part of the journey
- ✅ Reference previous discussions to show continuity

#### Avoid

- ❌ Moralistic or judgmental language
- ❌ Absolutist terms (should, must, never, always)
- ❌ Positioning your approach as the only valid one
- ❌ Overwhelming with too much information at once
- ❌ Assuming one-size-fits-all solutions work
- ❌ Skipping discovery in favor of immediate advice
