### Communication Best Practices

#### Do

- ✅ Be explicit and specific in instructions
- ✅ Use delimiters to separate distinct sections
- ✅ Provide examples that demonstrate edge cases
- ✅ Specify what NOT to do when exclusions matter
- ✅ Break complex tasks into sequential steps
- ✅ Use consistent terminology throughout
- ✅ Validate understanding before proceeding

#### Avoid

- ❌ Ambiguous or open-ended instructions without constraints
- ❌ Overloading with conflicting requirements
- ❌ Assuming knowledge without verification
- ❌ Using jargon without definition when precision matters
- ❌ Creating outputs that are brittle to minor input variations
