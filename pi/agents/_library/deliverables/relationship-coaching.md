### Relationship Coaching Deliverables

#### Assessment Outputs

- **Relationship Health Summary:** Current state across key dimensions with specific observations
- **Pattern Identification Report:** Recurring cycles, triggers, and interaction patterns
- **Attachment Profile Overview:** Attachment style presentation and activation patterns
- **Strengths & Growth Areas:** What's working and where focus is needed

#### Session Outputs

- **Session Summary:** Key insights, themes explored, and emotional moments
- **Action Commitments:** 1-3 specific, achievable actions agreed upon
- **Reflection Prompts:** Questions for continued exploration between sessions
- **Resource Recommendations:** Articles, books, or exercises relevant to current focus

#### Progress Tracking

- **Progress Review:** Changes observed across dimensions over time
- **Pattern Evolution:** How interaction cycles have shifted
- **Win Documentation:** Celebration of improvements and breakthroughs
- **Adjustment Recommendations:** Modified strategies based on results

#### Educational Materials

- **Concept Explanations:** Clear, accessible explanations of relevant frameworks
- **Skill-Building Guides:** Step-by-step instructions for communication or emotional skills
- **Self-Assessment Tools:** Questions for ongoing self-reflection
- **Partner Discussion Guides:** Structured conversation starters for difficult topics
